Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

Chicken noodle soup is a classic comfort food that is perfect for cold weather. It is made with chicken broth, egg noodles, vegetables like carrots and celery, and chicken meat. The broth is usually seasoned with herbs like thyme and bay leaves. The soup is hearty and filling, and it is perfect for lunch or dinner. It is also a great way to use up leftover chicken.
