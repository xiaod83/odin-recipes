Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a delicious and versatile dish that can be enjoyed as a main course or as a side dish. It is made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, meat, eggs, and seasonings. The result is a flavorful and satisfying dish that is perfect for any occasion. The aroma of the spices and the sizzle of the vegetables in the wok will make your mouth water. The combination of textures and flavors will leave you wanting more. It is a great way to use up leftover rice and vegetables in your fridge. Give it a try!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup chopped baby carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced(optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked white rice
  • 1 table spoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil

Steps

  1. Assemble ingredients.
  2. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in peas, then immediately drain in a colander.
  3. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds. Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.
  4. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
